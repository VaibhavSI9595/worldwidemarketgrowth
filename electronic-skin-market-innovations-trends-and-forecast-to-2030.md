<p><a href="https://www.sphericalinsights.com/reports/electronic-skin-market" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><strong>Electronic skin (E-skin)</strong></a>&nbsp;is an advanced, ultra-thin, flexible, and self-healing wearable technology that integrates telecommunications, nanotechnology, and sensor systems to mimic human or animal skin. It withstands stretching and flexing, making it ideal for prosthetics, AI, robotics, health monitoring, and medical implants. Driven by aging populations and chronic conditions, E-skin enhances healthcare by replacing bulky monitoring devices with flexible screens. Its self-healing properties, often from hybrid or polymer-based materials, are crucial for applications in soft robotics and biomedical implants.</p>
